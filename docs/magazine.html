Sneekie in print, 1988

Sneekie first reached the public as a type-in program in MS(X)DOS Computer Magazine — a Dutch magazine for PC and MSX users. Issue no. 25 ran a six-page feature on the game (pages 58–63): the write-up, a screenshot, and the complete GW-BASIC listing readers keyed in by hand, line by line.
