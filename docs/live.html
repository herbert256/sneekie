Eight copies of the real 1988 game, each running its own bot. Flip the switch to watch the gentle opening levels or the brutal back half — the self-moving levels where the snake grows roughly twice as long and every ♥ heart scatters a ♣ club into cleared ground. Each bot paths to the nearest target, pushes ◙ stones, dodges ↑←→ arrows, keeps a route home to its tail, and will eat a ☺ smiley to avoid boxing itself in. Clear a level and its screen flashes green and plays the same level again; get stuck and it flashes red and restarts.
